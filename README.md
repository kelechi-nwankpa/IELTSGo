# IELTSGo

AI-powered IELTS preparation platform with instant, examiner-style feedback.

---

## What is IELTSGo?

IELTSGo helps IELTS candidates prepare for all four test sections:

- **Writing** — Submit essays and get AI-powered feedback aligned with IELTS band descriptors
- **Speaking** — Record responses and receive evaluation on fluency, vocabulary, grammar, and pronunciation
- **Reading** — Practice with timed passages and get explanations for answers
- **Listening** — Complete exercises with audio and review detailed explanations

---

## Key Features

### AI-Powered Feedback (Writing & Speaking)

- Band estimates for each scoring criterion
- Specific, actionable suggestions referencing your actual response
- Feedback modeled on examiner standards

### Traditional Practice (Reading & Listening)

- Auto-scored with instant results
- AI explanations available on request
- Timed practice modes

### Personalized Study Plans

- Diagnostic-based recommendations
- Weekly/daily task suggestions
- Progress tracking
